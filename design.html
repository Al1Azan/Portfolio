Web Development
But Without Coding

Web development involves creating and maintaining websites, focusing on both aesthetics and functionality. It encompasses various tasks such as designing layouts, ensuring user-friendly navigation, and optimizing site performance. Developers often collaborate with designers to align visual elements with technical requirements. Key skills include understanding HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with familiarity with content management systems like WordPress. Additionally, knowledge of responsive design ensures websites function seamlessly across devices. Testing and debugging are crucial to identify and fix issues. Keeping up with industry trends and best practices enhances a developer's ability to create engaging, accessible, and effective web experiences for users.
